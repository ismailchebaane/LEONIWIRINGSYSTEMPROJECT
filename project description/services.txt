DocumentService.java: Handles file upload, retrieval, and deletion in MongoDB GridFS storage.

EquipmentServices.java: Manages equipment-related operations, including adding new equipment with documents, updating equipment details and documents, retrieving equipment, deleting equipment, and adding/removing documents from existing equipment.

UserServices.java: Manages user-related operations, including retrieving users, adding new users with validation, updating user details (username, matricule, work, role), and deleting users. It ensures uniqueness for username and matricule during updates.
